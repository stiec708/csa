Video Campaign OSFA
Brief
This was a group project created in collaboration with (Eileen Chen, Soobin Hong and Mathis Ekkebus.) Create a Brand that addresses the needs of an underserved community or group of people and create a promotional video, brand statement and logo that best represents it .
The Brand
OSFA(one size fits all) is a brand that seeks to create a true one size fits all by the use of adjustable elements and seeks to elevate the New York working womanâ€™s wardrobe. Here is its mission statement.
 
 
The OSFA Logo was created out of a custom font inspired by the signage on the New York City subway . Sleek and Modern it represents the look the OSFA woman strives to reach.
 
The Campaign
The campaign video showcases several outfits interchanging OSFA pieces to showcase their versatility. It was shot in the heart of SoHo to Capture the quintessential New York look and features a powerful female figure. (Ideally the campaign would bee expanded to feature several women with different shapes and sizess wearing OSFA but due to lack of availability we were unable to cast more model)
