Website Bagel Bonanza
Brief
Notice layers (nodes) in systems and how they function individually and together. Using shapes, images, styles, and typography, create a website inspired by layers found in nature or in the created world.
The Website
I chose to explore the relatively simple system of a bagel . The bagel its broken down into two layers, the filling and the bread and the website serves as a highly visual ordering platform for food that completely begets the need for Type, eliminating the language barrier involved in current food ordering apps.
