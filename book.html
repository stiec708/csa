The Book
 
‘Why You and I Relate To A Middle Aged Depressed Horse Named Bojack’ is a book that indirectly answers the question it is titled after by compiling information about Horses, Nihilism and the script to the first two episodes of Bojack Horseman. The Books is set up in so that the script runs along it’s interior with character defining lines from Bojack Highlighted and the exterior presents information about horses and nihilism in a more traditionally academic way. It was set up this way to give the impression off watching or reading the show in the background while reading the informational section
 
























The Cover
The Cover of the book reads ‘So a Horse Walks into your TV’. It is a play on the traditional joker opener so A Horse Walks into a Bar that utilises the two fonts used within the book to highlight its dual content format.
